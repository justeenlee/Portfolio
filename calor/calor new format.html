CALOR

Smart Shower Faucet

A simple, safe and water-saving shower

Calor is a smart shower faucet that safely and efficiently delivers your desired water temperature with integrated sensor technology. Through subtle suggestions and reward systems, it promotes a mindful water use and progressively guides you to a shorter shower time for water conservation.

Although faucet designs come out each year with new form and material reconsideration, the whole shower experience still remains inefficient and also reveals other problems.

Unpredictability

Due to inconsistent models and seasons, an average 2.7-time unnecessary adjustment causes time and water waste

Scalding

There are over 3000 scalding cases each year and over 90% incidents occur at home

Underestimated usage

Showers take up 17% of water households use in the U.S., 3rd usage after washing machines and toilets

Growing concern

More than 36 states are expecting water shortage in the next few years

In response to articles and news on California drought crisis (SF has already had the water saving campaign all over the city), saving water becomes more important and inevitable.

Facing these everyday hassles and public concerns, I set to envision the future shower experience and propose this flow:

As technologies become more ubiquitous and accessible in our lives, shower faucet should also take advantage of them and be smarter, personalized and more safe.

Market Potential

By disassembling a shower fixture, valve and cartridge, I understand how a faucet work behind the wall. Combining the market research findings, I go with a retrofitting solution that makes a tyical shower not only better, but afforable.

As the design centers around temperature control, the form need to reflect and become the representation of that interface. When imaginging what a faucet fixture could be like, I experiment with different combinations of forms that are enough to prompt the interaction without being too distracting.

While pushing boundries on forms, I draw connections to hand ergonomics and an existing pattern that we are already familiar with turning a nob/handle. As a result, I keep this natual and analogical turning interaction as key mechanism of controling water temperature.

Since turning, a radial movment, is the interaction method here, I choose a round over retangular display for temperature information, to reflect that symmetrial and circular motion.

I also combine the display and the turning ring into the same entity, where display is directly within the ring, to create greater intimacy between you and your water temperature.

Moving both fast and slowly, I refine and clean up the interface so that it could quickly tell sufficient information while not overwhelm with complexity.

To support a static display witin a moveable outer ring, I need to first look closely into the cartridge structure in a mechanical faucet, and devise a new mechanism for this inteaction.

Testing the feasibilty of a new mechanism with a 3D-printed cartidge prototype, I replicate the water-mixing mechanism that provides higher precision control driven by a motor.

I also prototype the temperature setting interaction, auto-calibration,and timer, with all intended sensors (rotary encoder and waterproof temperature sensor). Check out the code on Github.

Harness your smart phone

A survey I conducted shows that 1/3 of people interact with their phone or have in within close proximity, while they are waiting for hot water. Connecting them with their faucet and phone could benefit them in data viz and suggestions.

Transitioning from wireframes to mid-fidelity mockup still requires some additional iterations and tests to create a clear information hierarchy and consistent visual language.

Reward system to make every drop count

Water Bucket

The water bucket serves as an unobtrusive motivation that educates users to be mindful of water use and guides them to 5-min shower suggested by EPA.

Level Badges

Earning a bucket is easy at the beginning and gets more challenging. As you earn more, you will receive rare badges and see how your achievement stands among your friends or neighbourhood/community.

A system that transforms a typical shower experience into an efficient one that is more personalized, safe and predictable.
