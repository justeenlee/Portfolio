CALOR

Digital Faucet System

Efficient and reliable shower experience

Calor is a faucet system that safely and efficiently delivers your desired water temperature with a temperature sensor and promotes a mindful water use with integration technology. It’s smart to let you set your water temperature and suggest when to get out of shower.

Unpredictable Adjustment

Adjusting for 2.7 times in average causes unnecessary time, effort and water waste

Chance Of Burning

There are over 3000 scalding cases each year and over 90% incidents occur at home

Underestimated Water Usage

Average Americans use 17% for shower, 3rd usage after washing machines and toilets

A Growing Concern

More than 34 states are expecting water shortage in the next few years

In respond to recent articles and news on California drought crisis, saving water becomes more important and inevitable.

How to elevate shower experience while promote mindful water use?

Devise a system that captures a more efficient shower experience with sensor and integration technology

STREAMLINE TASK FLOW with temperature sensor

Calor benefits from a temperature sensor that results a perfect shower temperature you desire.

What you set is what you get. Transforming a typical shower experience into one that is more personalized, efficient, safe and predictable.

Disassembling an cartridge and valve gives me an idea of how they work and replacing only the cartridge instead of the entire faucet installation would have a more immediate impact.

With this attempt, I 3D printed one that could complete the same task but with the benefit of automation.

I create a quick mockup with a 24-neopixel ring and a 7-segment display that both connect to an arduino to visualize a possible interface of the count down. Check out the code on Github.
