About me

Hallo! I'm Zhendong Li, but I go by Justeen Lee.

I am an interaction and industrial designer who believes in useful yet emotional products, previously designing at MuleSoft, 1 of the 38 coolest startups in Silicon Valley.

Exploring sensor technology, analyzing human behavior, rendering pixels and manipulating keyframes together are my magic to visualize and bring new service and experience to life.

At the intersection of hardware and software, I develop my broad range of skills with focus on Internet of Things and Apps/Services with a design practice that begins with a system and service level to structure a meaningful experience framework and then fine-tunes emotional details with overlooked microinteraction and motion.
