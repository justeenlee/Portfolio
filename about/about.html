About me

Hallo! I'm Zhendong Li, but I go by Justeen Lee.

I am a Human Interaction Designer who believes products should be meaningful and carry experience loops of delight, surprise and emotion, previously at Pinterest.

With series of motion studies, arrays of sensor I/O's and lines of code, I define and prototype interactions among human, interfaces, devices and systems, visualizing and bringing new/aha moments right in front of people around me.
