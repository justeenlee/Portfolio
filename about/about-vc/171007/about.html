About me

Hallo! I'm Zhendong Li, but I go by Justeen Lee.

I am a Human Interaction Designer who believes products should be meaningful and carry experience loops of delight, surprise and emotion, previously at Pinterest.

With series of motion studies, arrays of sensor I/O's and lines of code, I define and prototype interactions between humans, interfaces, devices and systems. Believing in harmonious integration of hardware and software, I take the best part of each to visualize and bring new/aha moments to people around me.
