Bristol

Portable VR Viewer

Efficient and reliable shower experience

Bristol is portable VR viewer that is tailored to your style and offers a virtual reality experience by sliding in your phone with an pre-installed application.

Customizaiton and Portability

With the potential of VR and limitation of the original version of google cardboard, I aim to create:

A tailored VR experience for everyone, everywhere.

Jumping between sketching and modeling, I test out possible ideas with either method that could give me quicker feedbacks and results.

Accounting for tolerance for material thickness and fold line gives more precision and convenience for users to assemble. At last applying custom printed graphics on 11'' x 17'' label sheets generally completes this VR viewer making process.

How Bristol evolves: A series of iterations
