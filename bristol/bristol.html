Bristol

Portable VR Viewer

Tailored for everyone everywhere

Bristol is portable virtual reality viewer that extends the first version of Google Cardboard. Its customizable skin matches your style and the folding mechanism lets you to bring this immersive VR experience anywhere you go.

Customizaiton and Portability

With the potential of VR and limitation of the original version of google cardboard, I aim to:

Create a VR experience tailored for everyone, everywhere

Switching between sketching and modeling, I test out possible folding mechanisms until some “folding-flat” patterns begin to emerge.

Accounting for tolerance for material thickness and fold line gives more precision and convenience for users to assemble. At last applying custom printed graphics on 11'' x 17'' label sheets generally completes this VR viewer making process.

How Bristol evolves: A series of iterations

Make your own Bristol

To build your own Bristol, there is nothing much special but items you find can in a hardware store. Download the bristol flat pattern template and instructions.
