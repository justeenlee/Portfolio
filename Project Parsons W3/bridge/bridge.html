Bridge
Business Card Design

Bridge is a business card for interaction and product designers. It allows both visual and physical interaction to deliver a more elegant and joyful interaction through a simple fold.

Physical Interaction: Folding

"Can a business card be more joyful, not solely name and phone number?"

Visual Interaction: 3D Illustion

"I want to see more than a regular business card everyone hands to you."

Date: 2014
Client: Interaction & Product Designers
Team: Individual
State: Production
