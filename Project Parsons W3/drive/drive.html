Google Drive
Retouch on the download function

A quick hack and retouch on the download function in Google Drive to improve usability. For fun and hope see to some improvement in the real Google Drive in the future.

Another frequent task: Download

Sending out a survey to sample how users interact with the download function on Google Drive. Responses indicate download is one of the frequent functions users would perform.

Google Drive in 2014

New Google Drive was released in late 2014, and apparently had enhancement on both visual design and usability. However, I am surprised that there was no retouch on the download function.

1. Right click for menu

2. Scroll and Left Click download

More Streamlined and Intuitive

I feel for such a frequent function, the process should be more streamlined and intuitive.

1. Left Click to select

The download icon pops up as you select

2. Move over to download

Hit download without scrolling and switching buttons.

Another Alternative

Incorporating the download button on the menu bar creates a more cohesive visual language and also simplifies the case of downloading multiple files at once.
