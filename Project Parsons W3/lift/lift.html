Lift
iPhone 5 Case Packaging

Lift is an iPhone 5 case packaging designed for Material 6. More than just a packaging, it is also an amplifier for your iPhone 5/5s, which engages playful interactivity and promotes sustainability for dual-use.

How can I make a new packaging more elegant and interesting?

I set out to begin sketching basic forms and interactions, in which I try to draw some connections with one to another.

Then I explore the joints and structure of a packaging and seek a plausible one for my design.

With these shapes and volumes, one interesting thought comes to me:

What if the packaging is also an iPhone audio amplifier?

From here, I start folding and making models to test the viability of the structure and the amplifier idea.

After hours of trial and error, I have my first working prototype.

To test the hand fan engineering, I build another low fidelity prototype as a proof of concept, and then I move on to higher fidelity ones.

Finishing the structure of the packaging, I print a series of cover graphic to test out the first visual impression.

The amplifier comes in customizable colors and patterns.
