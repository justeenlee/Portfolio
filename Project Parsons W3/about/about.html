About me

Hallo! My name is Zhendong Li, but I go by Justeen Lee.

I spice up interaction with motion and microinteraction to connect people with a more engaging and meaningful experience.

I am an interaction designer with emphasis on interaction design, IoT and serivce design, currently studying Product Design and Computer Science at Georgia Tech.

Looking at the future as a hybrid of digital and physical realms, I land my focus on Internet of Things and Apps/Services with a practice of designing through a system and service scale to structure a more seamless meaningful overall experience framework, and then fine-tuning the user experience with overlooked microinteraction and motion.
