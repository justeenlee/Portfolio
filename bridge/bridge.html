Portal

Interactive Business Card

Portal is a business card for interaction and product designers. It allows both visual and tactile interaction to deliver a more elegant and joyful interaction through a simple fold.

Tangible Interaction: Folding

I want more than a regular business card everyone hands you, so I dig in further to transform into more delightful experience, rather than "oh it's another business card".
