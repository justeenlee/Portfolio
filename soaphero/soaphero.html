Soap Hero

Interactive Pediatric Waiting Room

Soap Hero is a game designed for the pediatric waiting room. It gives children a playful and interactive experience while they wait, and in addition to teach them some basic health knowledge.

Children just sit in a pediatric waiting room?

or
play with germs?

safe and fun waiting environment

Deliver a more engaging and playful experience for children without compromising on hygiene

They are ill children in the pediatric waiting room with age from 2 to 6 years old, and these two facts inform design imperatives.

what do they imply?

No Touching

Avoid spread of infections due to hand-touching

Less Movement

Avoid intense exercise before they see a doctor

Less Frustration

Avoid emotional impact of losing

Less Thinking

Avoid lots of information processing due to limited cognitive development and illness

Accommodate Multi-players

Allow but not require multiple players

Flexible Game Time

Come to play and leave as children need

Imperatives as filters

A narrow-down processing results a plausible concept

Now something has emerged: a game about catching dragonflies as a player steps on a pressure-sensitive tile.

Software
